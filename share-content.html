PreventionWeb publishes disaster risk reduction (DRR) content submitted by organizations working towards reducing disaster risk and building disaster resilience.

We do not publish individual work, academic theses, brochures, or advertising materials. For more information, see our content submission policy.

Share your disaster risk reduction work by selecting the type below:

  • Academic programmes

    Higher education, accredited programmes on or including disaster risk management and DRR leading to degrees or certificates at the Bachelors, Masters or PhD levels

    For non-credit training and courses, see Training & Events.

  • An existing network or a community

    Online listservs and communities of practice (CoPs) -- public or private -- promoting the exchange of information and experience in DRR

    Submit an existing network or CoP contact us to set one up.

  • Documents or publications

    Publication abstracts highlighting DRR with links to full text or publishers including books, case studies, good practice, guidelines, position papers, studies and reports

    For official governmental policy, see Policy, Plans & Statements For instructional guidelines and manuals, also see Educational Materials

  • Educational materials

    Multilingual instructional materials for use by teachers, trainers and community leaders to educate children and/or adults on DRR

    For non-educational materials, see Documents & Publications and other collections

  • Jobs

    Employment opportunities, internships and fellowship announcements including or focusing on DRR

    We do not accept CVs or applications - please see how to apply with the organization.

  • Maps

    Hazard frequency, risk and vulnerability maps

    We do not accept situation maps of current hazard and emergency events -- please visit ReliefWeb instead.

  • Multimedia

    Audio, video and interactive materials on DRR

  • News or announcements

    Global news coverage from media and organizations including press releases and announcements emphasizing DRR

  • Organizations in DRR

    A broad range of actors promoting or working in DRR

    Submit at least one example of your organization's work for publication, or submit your organization's DRR profile.

  • Policy, plans or statements

    Official governmental and intergovernmental policy, plans and statements on DRR including HFA progress reports on the implementation of the Hyogo Framework for Action and related development reports

    For policy or plans of organizations (not governmental), see Documents & Publications

  • Meetings and Conferences

    Meetings, conferences, seminars, and training courses (non-academic credit)

    For registration or additional event information, contact the organizers directly. For academic credit courses and training, see Academic Programmes.
