Risk Data Viewer (CAPRA)

GAR 2013

An interactive tool that visualizes the global risk data from the Global Assessment Report through maps and tables for download. At current, risk, hazard, exposure and events data is available for the following hazards: earthquake, cyclone winds and tsunami.

Global Risk Data Platform (Preview)

UNEP/GRID, UNISDR

An interactive tool to share spatial data information on global risk from natural hazards. Users can visualize, download or extract data on past hazardous events, human & economic hazard exposure and risk from natural hazards, including tropical cyclones and related storm surges, drought, earthquakes, biomass fires, floods, landslides, tsunamis and volcanic eruptions.
